Features


Food Suggestions

Food suggestion provides personalized recommendations based on the user's dietary needs and preferences.


Nutrition literature

Nutrition literature provides valuable information on the science of nutrition, dietary guidelines, and healthy eating practices.


Responsive

The application can be accessed and used effectively across a range of devices, including desktops, tablets, and mobile phones.


Affordable

The system is accessible to a wide range of users. It is free and available on a number of devices.


Timely reminder

Reminders help users stay on track with their nutrition goals by reminding them to eat at the right time.


Food analysis

Food analysis allows users to track and analyze the nutritional content of their food intake.
